# Utility Class Reference

This document lists all utility class patterns generated by the SCSS.

---

## LEGEND

- `[bp]` = Responsive breakpoint infix (e.g., `-sm`, `-md`, `-lg`, `-xl`, `-xxl`)
- `[num]` = Numeric value (e.g., 1-12 for cols, 0-5 for spacing)
- `[val]` = Keyword value (e.g., 'center', 'flex', 'auto')

---

## 1. LAYOUT

- `.app-container` - Main centered container with responsive padding.

---

## 2. GRID SYSTEM

- `.row` - Flexbox container for columns.

### Auto-sizing columns

- `.col` - Fills available space equally.
- `.col-[bp]` - e.g., `.col-md` (Fills space on medium screens and up)

### Numbered columns

- `.col-[num]` - e.g., `.col-6` (Spans 6 of 12 columns)
- `.col-[bp]-[num]` - e.g., `.col-lg-8` (Spans 8 of 12 columns on large screens and up)

---

## 3. DISPLAY

- `.d-[val]` - e.g., `.d-flex` (Sets `display: flex`)
- `.d-[bp]-[val]` - e.g., `.d-lg-none` (Sets `display: none` on large screens and up)
- **Values**: `none`, `block`, `flex`, `inline-flex`, `grid`, `inline-block`

---

## 4. FLEXBOX

### Direction

- `.flex-[val]` - e.g., `.flex-column` (Stacks flex items vertically)
- `.flex-[bp]-[val]` - e.g., `.flex-md-row` (Arranges items horizontally on medium screens and up)
- **Values**: `row`, `row-reverse`, `column`, `column-reverse`

### Grow & Shrink

- `.flex-grow-1` - Allows a flex item to grow to fill available space.
- `.flex-shrink-1` - Allows a flex item to shrink if needed.
- `.flex-[bp]-grow-1` - e.g., `.flex-lg-grow-1`

### Justify Content

- `.justify-content-[val]` - e.g., `.justify-content-between` (Distributes items evenly with space between)
- `.justify-content-[bp]-[val]` - e.g., `.justify-content-sm-center` (Centers items on small screens and up)
- **Values**: `start`, `end`, `center`, `between`, `around`, `evenly`

### Align Items

- `.align-items-[val]` - e.g., `.align-items-center` (Aligns items to the center of the container)
- `.align-items-[bp]-[val]` - e.g., `.align-items-xl-start` (Aligns items to the start on extra-large screens)
- **Values**: `start`, `end`, `center`, `baseline`, `stretch`

### Align Self

- `.align-self-[val]` - e.g., `.align-self-end` (Aligns a single item to the end)
- `.align-self-[bp]-[val]` - e.g., `.align-self-md-stretch` (Stretches item on medium screens and up)
- **Values**: `start`, `end`, `center`, `baseline`, `stretch`

---

## 5. SPACING

- **Values**: `0:0`, `1:4px`, `2:8px`, `3:16px`, `4:24px`, `5:48px`, `auto`

### Margin

- `.m-[num]` - e.g., `.m-3` (16px margin on all sides)
- `.mt-[num]` - e.g., `.mt-2` (8px margin-top)
- `.mx-[num]` - e.g., `.mx-auto` (Horizontally centers the element)
- `.m-[bp]-[num]` - e.g., `.m-lg-5` (48px margin on large screens and up)

### Padding

- `.p-[num]` - e.g., `.p-4` (24px padding on all sides)
- `.py-[num]` - e.g., `.py-3` (16px padding on top and bottom)
- `.px-[bp]-[num]` - e.g., `.px-md-5` (48px padding on left and right on medium screens and up)

### Gap

- `.gap-[num]` - e.g., `.gap-3` (16px gap between grid/flex items)
- `.gap-[bp]-[num]` - e.g., `.gap-lg-4` (24px gap on large screens and up)

---

## 6. SIZING

- `.w-100` - Sets `width: 100%`
- `.w-auto` - Sets `width: auto`
- `.w-[bp]-100` - e.g., `.w-md-100` (width: 100% on medium screens and up)

---

## 7. TYPOGRAPHY

### Font Size

- `.text-[size]` - e.g., `.text-lg` (18px), `.text-4xl` (36px)
- **Sizes**: `xs`, `sm`, `base`, `lg`, `xl`, `2xl`, `3xl`, `4xl`, `5xl`, `6xl`

### Font Weight

- `.fw-[weight]` - e.g., `.fw-bold` (700), `.fw-light` (300)
- **Weights**: `light`, `normal`, `medium`, `semibold`, `bold`, `black`

### Text Alignment & Wrapping

- `.text-[align]` - e.g., `.text-center`, `.text-start`
- **Alignments**: `start`, `end`, `left`, `right`, `center`, `justify`
- `.text-wrap` - Allows text to wrap.
- `.text-nowrap` - Prevents text from wrapping.

---

## 8. BORDERS & RADIUS

### Borders

- `.border` - Adds a border to all sides.
- `.border-top`
- `.border-end`
- `.border-bottom`
- `.border-start`
- `.border-0` - Removes border from all sides.
- `.border-top-0`
- `.border-end-0`
- `.border-bottom-0`
- `.border-start-0`

### Border Radius

- `.rounded` - Adds a default border radius.
- `.rounded-sm` - Small radius.
- `.rounded-lg` - Large radius.
- `.rounded-pill` - Pill-shaped radius.
- `.rounded-circle` - Circular radius.
- `.rounded-top`
- `.rounded-end`
- `.rounded-bottom`
- `.rounded-start`

---

## 9. SHADOWS

- `.shadow-sm` - Small box shadow.
- `.shadow` - Default box shadow.
- `.shadow-lg` - Large box shadow.
- `.shadow-none` - Removes box shadow.

---

## 10. POSITIONING

### Position

- `.position-[type]` - e.g., `.position-absolute`
- **Types**: `static`, `relative`, `absolute`, `fixed`, `sticky`

### Placement

- `.[prop]-[val]` - e.g., `.top-50`, `.start-0`, `.end-100`
- **Properties**: `top`, `bottom`, `start`, `end`
- **Values**: `0`, `50`, `100`

### Translate

- `.translate-middle` - Centers on both axes.
- `.translate-middle-x` - Centers on the x-axis.
- `.translate-middle-y` - Centers on the y-axis.

---

## 11. OVERFLOW

- `.overflow-[type]` - e.g., `.overflow-hidden`
- **Types**: `auto`, `hidden`, `visible`, `scroll`

---

## 12. INTERACTIVITY

### Opacity

- `.opacity-[val]` - e.g., `.opacity-50`
- **Values**: `0`, `25`, `50`, `75`, `100`

### Cursor

- `.cursor-pointer`
- `.cursor-not-allowed`
- `.cursor-grab`
